PUNIT GOUR - CEO of PactoraLuxe.in

Puneet Gaur is the Founder, Owner, and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Pectoral Lux Clothing Brand. At just 24 years old, he has built the brand with a vision of combining modern fashion, premium quality, and timeless style. Driven by creativity and a passion for clothing, Puneet founded Pectoral Lux to offer apparel that reflects confidence, individuality, and luxury. Under his leadership, Pectoral Lux has grown into a brand focused on delivering high-quality fashion that balances comfort, elegance, and contemporary trends. His commitment to innovation, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction continues to shape the company's identity and future growth. Puneet believes that clothing is more than just fabric—it is a statement of personality and self-expression. Through Pectoral Lux, he aims to inspire people to dress with confidence and embrace their unique style while enjoying premium craftsmanship and modern design.
