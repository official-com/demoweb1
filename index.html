Creative Director & Architect

Designing space, narrative, and structure.

I specialize in bridging the gap between editorial aesthetics and modern architectural principles, creating experiences that feel both grounded and expansive.

A philosophy of reduction.

Born in Copenhagen and currently based in London, my work is driven by a desire to distill complex ideas into their most elegant and understandable forms.

Over the past decade, I have collaborated with luxury brands, cultural institutions, and forward-thinking startups to define their visual language. My approach relies on premium typography, deliberate whitespace, and imagery that tells a story without saying a word.

It's not about adding elements until it looks full; it's about removing elements until nothing else can be taken away without breaking the design.
